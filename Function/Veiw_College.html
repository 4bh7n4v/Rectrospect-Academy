About IIT Rourkee

Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee is an autonomous research university in Roorkee, Uttarakhand. IIT Roorkee, which was founded in 1847 as Thomason College of Civil Engineering, is Asia's oldest technical institution. IIT Roorkee's main campus covers 358.5 acres in Roorkee, Haridwar District. IIT Roorkee has two more campuses: one in Saharanpur (about 30 km from Roorkee) and one in Greater Noida. IIT Roorkee NIRF Ranking 2024 has been released. The institute secured 8th position in the overall category, 6th in engineering, 1st in Architecture and Planning and 6th in innovation. The institute is known for its excellent placements. IIT Roorkee average package for MBA students in 2023 was Rs 18.34 LPA. IIT Roorkee has 23 departments and provides 14 UG, 24 PG, and various PhD programmes. The most popular courses of the institute are B.Tech and B.Arch. JEE Advanced scores are accepted for BTech admission.




IIT Roorkee Placements

Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee’s Department of Management Studies (DOMS) has released the IIT Roorkee MBA placement report 2021-23 on the official website. This year, the average CTC offered to students has been increased from Rs 16.8 LPA to Rs 18.3 LPA. The institute has achieved a 100% placement record in various sectors namely Analytics, Consulting, BFSI, FMCG, ITES, Retail, Manufacturing and Logistics. The number of companies visited during this placement season was 50+ corporates including new recruiters. DOMS has notified that this year, the median package offered was Rs 18.09 LPA, while the Indian Institute of Technology lowest package stood at Rs 11.6 LPA. IIT Roorkee highest package offered was Rs. 27.94 LPA.
