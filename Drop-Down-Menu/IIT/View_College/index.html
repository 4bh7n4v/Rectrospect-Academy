About Indian Institutes of Technology

The Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) are premier autonomous public technical and research universities in India, recognized as Institutes of National Importance. Renowned globally for their academic rigor, innovative research, and contributions to science and technology, the IITs are synonymous with excellence in engineering, technology, and management education.
